## Exercise
Perform bitwise operations on integer variables

## Reference Solution
```python
a = 5   # 101 in binary
b = 3   # 011 in binary

print("Bitwise AND of", a, "and", b, "is:", a & b)
print("Bitwise OR of", a, "and", b, "is:", a | b)
print("Bitwise XOR of", a, "and", b, "is:", a ^ b)
print("Bitwise NOT of", a, "is:", ~a)
print("Left shift of", a, "by 1 is:", a << 1)
print("Right shift of", a, "by 1 is:", a >> 1)
```
